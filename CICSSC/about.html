About Student Council Hub

Learn more about our platform and mission

Our Mission

The Student Council Hub is designed to bridge the communication gap between the student council and the student body. Our mission is to create a transparent, accessible, and efficient platform for sharing information, announcements, and resources.

What We Do

We provide a centralized digital space where students can:

  • Stay updated with the latest announcements from the student council
  • Access official documents, resolutions, and letters
  • Learn about upcoming events and programs
  • Review student council rules and guidelines
  • Engage with the student governance process

Our Vision

We envision a campus where every student has easy access to information about student governance and can actively participate in the decision-making processes that affect their educational experience.

Features

Real-time Announcements

Instant updates on important notices and news from the student council.

Document Repository

Centralized storage for all official student council documents.

Event Management

Comprehensive calendar of student council programs and events.

Role-based Access

Different permissions for admins and students to ensure proper information flow.
