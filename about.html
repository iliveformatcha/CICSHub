About Student Council Hub

Empowering student governance through technology

Our Mission

The Student Council Hub is designed to bridge the communication gap between the student council and the student body. We believe in transparent, efficient, and accessible student governance.

What We Offer

Instant Announcements

Real-time updates and important notices from your student council.

Event Management

Comprehensive calendar and event planning tools for all student activities.

Document Center

Centralized repository for all official documents, resolutions, and guidelines.

Feedback System

Direct communication channel between students and the council.

Our Team

This platform was developed by students, for students. We understand the unique challenges of student governance and have built solutions that address real needs.

Technology

Built with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, the Student Council Hub ensures a seamless experience across all devices while maintaining data security and privacy.
